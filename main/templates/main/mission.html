our mission

To build an inclusive community through advocating for and maintaining publicly accessible skateparks, skatepark stewardship, initiating and supporting skate programs, and creating mentorship and support for all

vision

Skate XP envisions a world where the sound of Skateboarding = the Sound of Success. Skateboarding is an individualized activity that builds lasting friendships and community. The Skateboard Experience fosters these connections and broadens their scope, providing access for skateboarders into their surrounding communities, granting them opportunities for personal growth, career opportunities, and achieving individualized long-term success.
