p.e. program

Skate XP is stoked to be offering Berkeley High school students a PE waiver for skateboarding. Sign-up today and start earning physical education credits for Berkeley High school students! Skate XP BHS PE Waiver Program is a wonderfully mixed group of 9th and 10th graders with a wide range of skateboarding ability. It is important to us that everyone feels welcome. Scholarships available.

skate spotters

Skate XP is working to help create new skate spots and parks for the community in the East Bay. We are currently gathering input on designs for a skate spot at James Kenney Recreation center in Berkeley and looking for ways to expand the skateboarding experience. Get in touch to see how you can help expand the opportunities for the local skateboarders.

hot doggers

A program designed for the experienced younger skater – one who already has a solid ollie, some grinding ability, and is hungry to progress and be challenged both as an individual and as a member of the hot doggers. The hot doggers meet twice a week at various skate spots/skate parks. Get-togethers are mentored by a skate XP staff member who is qualified to bring “the stoke“ and motivate skaters to move forward in skateboarding, and in life.
