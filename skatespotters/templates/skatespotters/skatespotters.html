Skate spotters

Skate spotters is a group of local skaters organized by Skate XP to promote development of new skate spots and improve existing skateparks in Berkeley, Oakland, and surrounding cities. Our current activities include developing plans for a skatespot at the James Kenney recreational Center (Berkeley), working on improvements at the Bella Vista Skatespot (Oakland), contributing skate obstacles to San Antonio Park (Oakland), advocating for improvements at the Berkeley Skatepark, and activating the skateboarding (skating) community to be engaged in the process of creating new skate spots and parks.

We communicate with city officials in order to raise awareness on the benefits of skating and the needs of skaters, and to identify ways to develop new skate facilities. If these activities interest you, we need your help, so please join us.
